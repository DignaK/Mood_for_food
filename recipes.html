Peach, Mango + Carrot Baby Food Puree

This Peach, Mango + Carrot Baby Food Puree is filled with peaches, mango, carrots and mint for a taste sensation. Great for 6+ months.

    Ingredients
  • 2 peaches, pitted and roughly chopped (frozen works)
  • 2 mangos, peeled, pitted and roughly chopped (frozen works)
  • 4 carrots, peeled and roughly chopped
  • 4-6 leaves mint, roughly chopped
  • 1/2 cup liquid, water, breast milk, formula or stock
    Instructions
  1. Place the carrots and liquid into a medium saucepan, cover, and heat over medium heat for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. Add in the peaches and mangos, cover and cook for 3 additional minutes or until carrots can easily be pricked with a fork. Let cool slighly.
  3. Transfer all of the ingredients into a blender or food processor. Add in the mint and puree for 1-2 minutes or until you have reached your desired consistency, adding more liquid if needed in 1/4 cup increments.
